Sean McBride

I'm a postdoctoral research fellow at the University of British Columbia. I got my PhD in 2024 from UC Santa Barbara, advised by Xi Dong. I study quantum gravity and string theory, but I am broadly interested in topics in black hole physics, conformal field theory, and condensed matter physics.

In the far-off past, I've done experimental work in accelerator physics, plasma physics, and chemistry.
