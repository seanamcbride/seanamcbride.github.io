Mjedra

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown lentils
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 large white onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup long-grain rice
  • Salt, pepper, and cumin to taste

Instructions

Saut√© onions in the olive oil with salt, pepper, and a healthy shake of cumin. Rinse the lentils, add to the water with salt and pepper, bring to a boil, cover and cook until the lentils are tender, about 20 minutes. If you start these steps at the same time the onions should be caramelized when the lentils are cooked. Add the onions, oil, and uncooked rice to the lentils and simmer for another 20 minutes, until all the water is absorbed and the rice is cooked. Season at the end with salt, pepper, and cumin to taste.
